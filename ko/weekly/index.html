The Institute runs a weekly rhythm of public sessions, learning groups, and project meetings. The most current view of what's happening this week is the public Calendar, which lists upcoming sessions soonest-first — so this week's events appear at the top — and lets you search and filter to find a specific session.

This week on the calendar

The public Calendar is the single source for what is happening this week. Upcoming events are listed soonest-first, so the sessions running this week sit at the top of the list, and you can use the calendar's on-page search and the upcoming/past filter to narrow to a particular session or kind of event. Because the calendar is generated from the Institute's live event feed, it stays current as sessions are added or change.

The Institute's weekly rhythm

Across a typical week the Institute hosts recurring meetings, learning-group sessions, and project sessions, open to participants. These recurring activities are the day-to-day expression of the Institute's programs, and the Activities page gives a fuller picture of the kinds of sessions that make up the rhythm beyond any single week.
