2025 is the Active Inference Institute's annual overview for the year — a consolidated view of activities, programs, and direction across the organization. It brings together the work of the Institute's three program units under the motto Act. Infer. Serve. The detailed goals, milestones, and event record for the year are maintained on the 2025 hub and are being migrated onto this site.

A Year in Review

This page gathers the Institute's 2025 activity into a single annual overview. Rather than tracking any one project, it offers a consolidated view of how education, research, and operations advanced together over the year. As a participatory, open-science nonprofit, the Institute conducts its work in public, and this overview points toward the records, materials, and outputs produced across the year. The authoritative and continuously updated record of specific goals, milestones, and events lives on the 2025 hub.

Across the Three Program Units

The Institute carries out its work through three program units, and 2025 reflects the contributions of each. EduActive supports the Institute's education programs, ReInference supports its research, and the Administrative unit supports operations that hold the whole together. Reviewing the year through these units shows how learning, inquiry, and stewardship reinforce one another. For the structure that organizes this work, see the Institute structure overview.

Direction and Participation

An annual overview is also a chance to look forward. The Institute is participatory by design, and its direction emerges from the people who take part across learning groups, projects, and operations. Anyone interested in contributing to the year's ongoing work — or to what comes next — can find current pathways through the Institute's participation programs.

기관 소개를 찾는 방문객들. 활성 추론 연구소 소개

액티브 인퍼런스 연구소(Active Inference Institute)는 액티브 인퍼런스의 접근성, 엄격성 및 적용성을 향상시키는 것을 목표로 하는 자원봉사자 중심의 비영리 단체입니다. 다양한 배경, 시간대 및 숙련도를 가진 사람들을 환영합니다.

 동기적으로 학습하고 기여할 방법을 찾는 참여자들 활동

활동은 연구소의 실시간 참여 표면입니다: 학습 그룹, 모델 스트리밍, 프로젝트 회의, 공개 미디어, 그리고 정기 업데이트.

 기관의 조직과 운영 방식에 대해 이해하고자 하는 방문객들을 위한 내용입니다. 연구소 구조

Active Inference Institute는 이사회, 집행 임원진, 과학 자문 위원회 및 교육과 연구 두 개의 운영 부서로 구성됩니다.
