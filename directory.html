About the Institute

The Active Inference Institute is a volunteer-led 501(c)(3) open science nonprofit making Active Inference known, taught, and applied across science, engineering, and practical domains.

PurposeMission, Vision, Values, and PrinciplesInstitute StructureInformation Surfaces

Active Inference

Active Inference is the central scientific and practical framework around which the Institute organizes education, research, applications, and ecosystem support.
