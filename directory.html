About the Institute

The Active Inference Institute is a participatory open science institute dedicated to improving the accessibility, rigor, and applicability of the Active Inference framework.

PurposeMission, Vision, Values, and PrinciplesInstitute StructureInformation Surfaces

Active Inference

Active Inference is the central scientific and practical framework around which the Institute organizes education, research, applications, and ecosystem support.

What Active Inference ProvidesHow to Approach the FieldImplementationsApplications
