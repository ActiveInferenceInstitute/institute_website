The Officers of the Active Inference Institute are responsible for executing the Institute's day-to-day operations, administration, and finances under the governance of the Board of Directors.

Role of the Officers

Officers carry out the operational, administrative, and financial functions of the Active Inference Institute. They implement Board-approved strategy and policy, manage records and finances, and coordinate the Institute's programs and units. Officers are appointed under the Bylaws and serve alongside the Board of Directors and the Scientific Advisory Board.
