The Active Inference Institute is a registered 501(c)(3) nonprofit. Donations from individuals and organizations directly fund public education, research infrastructure, community events, and ecosystem stewardship.

Why Donate

The Active Inference Institute operates as an open-science nonprofit. All of our educational materials, research outputs, software, and community resources are freely available to the public. This work requires infrastructure, coordination, and sustained effort that philanthropic support makes possible. Donations help ensure that Active Inference education and research remains accessible to anyone, regardless of institutional affiliation or geographic location.

Donation pageSupport

What Your Donation Supports

Philanthropic contributions fund: operational infrastructure for the Institute and its community platforms; audio-visual production for livestreams, podcasts, and educational recordings; event organization for the Applied Active Inference Symposium and other gatherings; educational program development including textbook groups, courses, and workshops; support for interns, fellows, and volunteers; and maintenance of shared computational and storage resources.

Tax Deductibility

The Active Inference Institute is incorporated in Delaware, USA as a 501(c)(3) educational nonprofit organization. Donations from US taxpayers are tax-deductible to the extent permitted by law. Donors receive a receipt acknowledging their contribution. Please consult your tax advisor for guidance on deductibility in your specific situation.

Organizational Giving

Organizations that wish to provide larger gifts or ongoing support may do so through a combination of philanthropic giving and formal partnership. If your organization is interested in providing significant philanthropic support, reach out to discuss how your contribution can best serve the Institute's mission and your organization's goals.

Partnership

Other Ways to Support

Beyond direct donation, you can support the Institute by volunteering your time, contributing to open-source projects, sharing Institute resources with your networks, and participating in community activities. Every form of contribution — time, expertise, and financial — helps sustain the work.

Institute Programs turn interest into participation through volunteer, internship, mentorship, fellowship, partnership, philanthropy, grants, and open-source pathways. All programs are open to people of any background and familiarity level.

 Organizations, research groups, universities, companies, and institutions interested in formal collaboration with the Institute. Partnership

Partnerships align organizations and collaborators around concrete shared work — co-hosting events, co-authoring research, sharing infrastructure, or developing joint education and application programs.

 Researchers, educators, and project teams seeking funding for Active Inference work. Grants

The Institute supports grant proposals and collaborative funding efforts that advance Active Inference research, education, and application. Participants and projects can seek Institute support for proposal preparation, institutional affiliation, and co-investigation.

 Visitors seeking an institutional orientation. About the Institute

The Active Inference Institute is a volunteer-led 501(c)(3) nonprofit dedicated to improving the accessibility, rigor, and applicability of Active Inference. We welcome people of all backgrounds, time zones, and familiarity levels.
