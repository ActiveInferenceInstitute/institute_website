A Year in Review

This page gathers the Institute's 2025 work into a single annual overview. Rather than tracking any one project, it offers a consolidated view of how education, research, and operations advanced together over the year. As a participatory, open-science 501(c)(3) nonprofit, the Institute conducts its work in public, so this overview is best read as a map: it orients you to the records, materials, and outputs produced through the year and shows where to look for specifics. The authoritative, continuously updated record of goals, milestones, and events lives on the 2025 hub.

Across the Three Program Units

The Institute carries out its work through three program units, and the year reflects the contribution of each. EduActive supports the Institute's education programs, ReInference supports its research, and the Administrative unit supports the operations that hold the whole together. Reading the year through these units shows how learning, inquiry, and stewardship reinforce one another rather than running on separate tracks. For the structure that organizes this work and how the units relate, see the Institute structure overview.

Working in the Open

Open science is not a slogan here but a working method, and the year reflects it. The Institute's outputs — research, educational materials, and the conversations around them — are produced so that others can find, follow, and build on them. That is what makes an annual overview possible at all: the year is legible because so much of it happened in public. This page consolidates the threads, while the underlying materials remain distributed across the programs and projects that generated them.

Direction and Participation

An annual overview is also a chance to look forward. The Institute is participatory by design, and its direction emerges from the people who take part across learning groups, projects, and operations. The year's momentum carries into what comes next, and the paths to contribute stay open between years rather than resetting. Visitantes en busca de una orientación institucional. Sobre el Instituto

El Instituto de Inferencia Activa es una organización sin fines de lucro tipo 501(c)(3) liderada por voluntarios dedicada a mejorar la accesibilidad, el rigor y la aplicabilidad de la Inferencia Activa. Damos la bienvenida a personas de todos los orígenes, zonas horarias y niveles de familiaridad.

 Participantes en busca de formas sincrónicas de aprender y contribuir. Actividades

Las actividades son la superficie de participación en vivo del Instituto: grupos de aprendizaje, transmisiones de modelos, reuniones de proyectos, medios públicos y actualizaciones recurrentes.

 Visitantes que buscan comprender cómo está organizado y gobernado el Instituto. Estructura del Instituto

El Instituto de Inferencia Activa está organizado en torno a una Junta Directiva, oficiales ejecutivos, una Junta Asesora Científica y dos unidades operativas para educación e investigación.
